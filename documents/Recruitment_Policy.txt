ABC Technologies Pvt. Ltd.
Recruitment Policy
Version 1.0

Purpose
Defines standardized hiring practices to attract, assess, and onboard qualified candidates fairly.

Scope
Applies to all permanent, contract, and internship recruitment activities.

Workforce Planning
Hiring managers submit manpower requests with business justification and budget approval.

Job Requisition
HR reviews approved requisitions before publishing job openings.

Candidate Sourcing
Candidates may be sourced through job portals, referrals, campus hiring, agencies, and internal postings.

Selection Process
Resume screening, technical assessments, interviews, reference checks, and final approval.

Offer & Background Verification
Offers are conditional upon successful background verification and document validation.

Probation
New employees complete the probation period before confirmation, subject to satisfactory performance.

Equal Employment Opportunity
Recruitment decisions are based on merit without unlawful discrimination.

Record Retention
Recruitment records are maintained securely according to company policy.
