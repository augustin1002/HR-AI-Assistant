ABC Technologies Pvt. Ltd.
Code of Conduct
Version 1.0

Purpose
This Code of Conduct establishes ethical, professional, and legal standards expected of all employees.

Core Values
Integrity, Respect, Accountability, Innovation, Collaboration, and Customer Focus.

Professional Behaviour
Employees must treat colleagues, customers, and partners respectfully and professionally.

Equal Opportunity
The company prohibits discrimination based on race, gender, age, religion, disability, or any protected characteristic.

Anti-Harassment
Harassment, bullying, intimidation, and retaliation are prohibited. Employees should report concerns immediately.

Dress Code
Employees are expected to maintain professional attire appropriate to their role and workplace.

Confidentiality
Confidential company and customer information must not be disclosed without authorization.

Conflict of Interest
Employees must avoid situations where personal interests conflict with company interests.

IT & Social Media
Company systems are for business purposes. Social media posts must not disclose confidential information.

Disciplinary Action
Violations may result in counseling, written warnings, suspension, or termination depending on severity.

Reporting Misconduct
Employees may report concerns to HR, their manager, or the ethics committee without fear of retaliation.

Revision History
