ABC Technologies Pvt. Ltd.
IT Security Policy
Version 1.0

Purpose
This policy defines the minimum information security requirements to protect company data, systems, and technology assets.

Scope
Applies to all employees, contractors, interns, consultants, and third parties who access company IT resources.

Password Policy
Passwords must be at least 12 characters long, unique, and changed immediately if compromise is suspected. Password sharing is prohibited.

Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA)
MFA is mandatory for company email, VPN access, cloud services, and other critical business applications.

Acceptable Use
Company devices and networks are primarily for business purposes. Users must not install unauthorized software or access malicious websites.

Email & Phishing Security
Employees must verify suspicious emails, avoid clicking unknown links, and immediately report phishing attempts to the IT Security team.

Data Classification
Information is classified as Public, Internal, Confidential, or Restricted. Sensitive information must be stored and transmitted securely.

Device Security
All company laptops and mobile devices must use full-disk encryption, antivirus protection, and automatic screen locking.

Remote Work Security
Employees working remotely must use the company VPN, secure Wi-Fi, and approved collaboration tools.

USB & Removable Media
Use of personal USB drives is prohibited unless specifically approved by the IT department.

Incident Reporting
Security incidents, lost devices, suspected malware, or unauthorized access must be reported to IT immediately.

Disciplinary Action
Violations of this policy may result in disciplinary action, including termination of employment and legal action where applicable.
