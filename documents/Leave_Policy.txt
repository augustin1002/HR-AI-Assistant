Employee Leave Policy
Version 1.0

Purpose
This policy defines employee leave entitlements, approval procedures, compliance requirements, and responsibilities to support employee wellbeing and business continuity.

Scope
Applies to full-time employees. Interns and contractors follow their contract terms unless otherwise specified.

Leave Types
Casual Leave: 8 days/year. Sick Leave: 7 days/year. Earned Leave: 24 days/year. Maternity Leave: 26 weeks. Paternity Leave: 10 days. Bereavement Leave: 5 days.

Leave Approval
Employees submit requests through the HRMS or email. Reporting manager approves. HR verifies balances and updates records.

Carry Forward
Earned Leave may be carried forward up to 60 days. Sick Leave may be carried forward up to 21 days. Casual Leave lapses at year end.

Leave Encashment
Only eligible Earned Leave may be encashed according to company policy and applicable law.

Attendance
Employees should notify managers before shift start if absent. Unauthorized absence may lead to disciplinary action.

Responsibilities
Employees apply in advance and provide documents when required. Managers ensure timely approval. HR maintains records.

FAQs
Q: Can I combine leave types? A: Yes, subject to approval.
Q: Is a medical certificate required? A: Yes, for extended sick leave.
