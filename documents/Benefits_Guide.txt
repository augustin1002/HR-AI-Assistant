Employee Benefits Guide
Version 1.0

Overview
This guide outlines the employee benefits available to eligible employees.

Health Insurance
Comprehensive medical insurance for employees with dependent coverage subject to plan terms.

Provident Fund
Eligible employees participate in the statutory provident fund scheme.

Gratuity
Gratuity is provided according to applicable employment laws and company policy.

Performance Bonus
Annual performance bonuses may be awarded based on company and individual performance.

Allowances
Travel, meal, mobile, and internet reimbursements are available for eligible roles.

Learning & Development
Employees receive access to training, certifications, workshops, and learning platforms.

Employee Wellness
Wellness initiatives include health checkups, counseling support, and fitness activities.

Retirement Benefits
Retirement-related benefits are administered according to company policy and legal requirements.

Employee Assistance Program
Confidential support services are available for personal and work-related challenges.

Eligibility
Benefit eligibility may vary by employment type, grade, and length of service.
